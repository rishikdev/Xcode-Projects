About
RYTD (Return YouTube Dislike) brings dislikes back to YouTube!
How to Use RYTD
If you have enabled the extension, then you will see the dislike count right beside the dislike button!

Note: Sometimes, the dislike count from a previous video may carry over. In such cases, refreshing the page should display the correct dislike count.
